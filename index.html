Our Story

Born in Pakistan,
Built for the World

mySRZ Tourism was founded in 2014 by a group of mountaineers, historians, and food lovers who were tired of watching international visitors miss the real Pakistan. They had watched tourists stick to Islamabad's Centaurus Mall while K2 base camp, the Lahore Food Street, and the Kalash festivals went undiscovered.

Today we are a team of 40+ guides, writers, and logistics experts, each specialists in their home region. We have taken over 15,000 travellers to places that still feel like secrets.
